Exploration of Museum Rooms

By pressing the "H" key on the keyboard throughout the tour of the museum, the help screen appears.

This screen gives us information on how the user, can move through the application's environment, both indoors and outdoors. This is done as follows:

Pressing the "W" key, to move forward.
Pressing the "S" key to move backwards.
Pressing the "A" key to move left.
Pressing the 'D' key to move right.

By pressing the keys in a similar way the user can move and enter the museum areas for the first time. It is also possible to close this window by pressing the "Q" key, as indicated at the top right of the screen.
