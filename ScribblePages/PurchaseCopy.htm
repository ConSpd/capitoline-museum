Purchase a Copy

The application has been designed and developed in such a way that it gives the user the option to buy an exact copy of any exhibit by approaching the exhibit and pressing the "P" button.

Verification of this choice can be made by then pressing the "F" key, so that the user's cart at the current time is also displayed.

The copy has been added to the user's cart, and with the cost of purchasing the copy being displayed in the lower left of the window relating to the cart. At this point, it is worth highlighting the fact that the user, at any time, has the possibility to remove a copy from his/her basket and not proceed with its purchase (by clicking on the "X" next to the name of the exhibit).
