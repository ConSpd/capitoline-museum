Completion of purchase (Check - Out)

The user, having now completed his/her tour of the museum and before leaving, can go back to the Reception area in order to complete the purchase of the copies of the exhibits he/she liked the most and chose to add to his/her basket.

To the available options provided by the Receptionist of the museum, another one has been added, through which the CHECK - OUT procedure will be carried out. (This presupposes that the user's basket is not empty, i.e. that he has added at least one copy of an exhibit to be purchased.)

By pressing the "4" button, he will be asked to add his card details.

And then, it will be redirected and the corresponding window will appear to enter the data. Once the correct data entry is completed and the user presses "CONFIRM PAYMENT", the purchase is successfully completed and the Receptionist thanks the user for the purchase he has just made.
